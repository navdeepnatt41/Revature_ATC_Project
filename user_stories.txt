    GET:
    - As a user, I want to be able to see all active flights in my city. 
    - As a flight dispatcher, I want to view all scheduled flights for a specific route so I can manage flight assignments efficiently.
    - As a customer service agent, I want to look up route details including origin and destination airports to answer passenger queries.
    - As a flight crew member, I want to see my upcoming scheduled flights.
    - As HR, I want to track employee positions and statuses to ensure compliance with regulations.
    - As a maintenance manager, I want to view all aircraft by manufacturer and model to schedule maintenance.
    - As an in-flight employee/pilot, I want to view my upcoming flight assignments including supervisor and airline details.
    - As a passenger, I want to see complete airport information (name, city, country) for my departure and arrival locations.

    POST:
    - As a scheduler, I want to assign aircraft to flights based on availability and route requirements.
    - As a network planner, I want to be able to schedule new flights.
    
    PUT:
    - As an operations manager, I want to update flight status in real-time so passengers and crew receive accurate information.

    DELETE:
    - As a operations manager, I want to remove airlines that are no longer in businesses
    - As a scheduler, I want to remove routes that are no longer being flown
    - As an employee, I want to quit
    - As a scheduler, I want to cancel flights based on availability and/or weather events
    - As an inspector, I want to remove a plane from service if it is to old and in disrepair
